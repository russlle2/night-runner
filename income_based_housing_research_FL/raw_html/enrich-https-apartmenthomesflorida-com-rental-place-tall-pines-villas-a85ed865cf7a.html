City Description

Eustis is a city in Lake County, Florida, United States. The population was 18,558 at the 2010 census. The Census Bureau estimated the population in 2019 to be 21,303. It is part of the Orlando–Kissimmee–Sanford Metropolitan Statistical Area. Eustis High School is the town's local public high school. The City of Eustis holds a festival every year which begins on the last Friday of February and runs through Sunday and has been held since 1902. This festival, referred to simply as GeorgeFest, is recognized today as the second longest ongoing annual event held in honor of George Washington, first President of the United States. The median income for a household in the city was $32,032.
