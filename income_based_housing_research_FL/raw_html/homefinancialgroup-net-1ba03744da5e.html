USDA Mortgage Details for Sumter County Homebuyers

The USDA Rural Development loan program offers one of the most compelling value propositions in residential mortgage financing — zero down payment and below-market interest rates for eligible properties and borrowers in Sumter County, Florida. Contrary to popular belief, USDA-eligible areas are not limited to farmland; many suburban communities throughout Sumter County qualify based on population density criteria.

USDA loans are available to moderate-income borrowers who meet household income limits set by the U.S. Department of Agriculture for Sumter County. The annual mortgage insurance fee for USDA loans is just 0.35% — significantly lower than FHA's 0.55% or conventional PMI rates — which helps keep monthly payments affordable even without a down payment. With the median home price in Sumter County at $350,000, the USDA program puts homeownership within reach for buyers who might otherwise struggle to save a traditional down payment.

Our loan officers are familiar with the USDA property eligibility maps and income limit tables for the area and can provide a fast eligibility determination. If you qualify, a USDA loan may be the most affordable path to homeownership in Sumter County.
