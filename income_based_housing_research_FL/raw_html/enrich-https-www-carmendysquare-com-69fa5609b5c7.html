Charming Living in the heart of Florida

Spacious Town Homes with the Convenience of Apartment Living

Carmendy Square Townhomes in Lady Lake, FL is a charming townhome rental community with convenient access to Highway 441, making your commute effortless. We are proudly located within the Lake County Public School District, with UF Health Leesburg Hospital and UF Health Spanish Plaines Hospital just a short drive from our homes for rent. As a resident, you'll be minutes from The Villages' Lake Sumter Landing Market Square and Spanish Springs Town Square, offering exclusive shopping, dining, and entertainment options. Enjoy local attractions like Old Mill Playhouse and Spanish Springs Lanes Bowling Alley, just moments from your new rental home.

The Neighborhood

Situated just over 50 miles north of Orlando, our charming rental home community in Lady Lake is outside of the hustle and bustle of Orlando city life. Surrounded by 250 stunning lakes, the fun a tranquility of outdoor Florida living is at your fingertips.
